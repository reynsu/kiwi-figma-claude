How do I accept an invitation?

When someone invites you to connect on KiwiChat, you'll receive a notification in the app. To accept it, tap on the Notifications tab at the bottom of your screen.

You'll see a pending invitation with the person's name and facility. Tap Accept to confirm — they'll be added to your contacts list and you can start chatting right away.

If you don't want to connect, you can tap Decline and the invitation will be removed.
